General

7Breaths is a mobile application designed to aid in clinically determining the respiratory rate of a patient. The original idea was conceived by Wai Keong on the NHS HackDay mailing list and his blog. Barry Rowlingson first suggested the application name.

Clinical Software Solutions have implemented the application. The full source has been open sourced and is available in our GitHub repository at https://github.com/ClinicalSoftwareSolutions/7breaths

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy relates to the application binaries released by Clinical Software Solutions into various app stores. Forked versions of the application may have had their source modified.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application. Registration with us is optional and is used to understand our user base and also to provide you with feedback about the application and results of any data collected.

When you register with us and use the Application, you generally provide (a) your name, email address, occupation details, user name, password and other registration information.

The application has been released as an aid to respiratory rate calculation but also as a research tool to answer some questions about the clinical process of counting a person's respiratory rate.

After a respiratory rate has been calculated you will be prompted to submit the data to our data collection service. We request that you only submit data on real patients. No patient details are collected. The information submitted will include:

  • Each of the timestamps for a breath taken
  • The mode the application was used in
  • A unique device ID. This DOES NOT identify you or the patient in anyway and is purely a method of aggregating results from single users
The information collected will be aggregated and analysed for public consumption.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

All of this data is anonymous and aggregated. Single device usage is not collected.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect any information about the location of your mobile device.
